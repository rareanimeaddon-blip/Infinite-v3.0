---
name: AnimeSalt LG TV video freeze fix
description: Root cause and fix for LG WebOS video freezing while audio plays on AnimeSalt streams.
---

## Rule

For AnimeSalt CDN multi-quality master streams (`isVariant = false` path in `computeRelayM3u8`), **strip all `#EXT-X-MEDIA:TYPE=AUDIO` renditions and `AUDIO="..."` attributes** from the proxied outer master before returning it.

**Why:** LG WebOS HLS player cannot handle a stream where the video TS is muxed (already contains an audio PID — Hindi PID 256 in the CDN segments) AND the HLS master also declares an external `AUDIO=` group. The two audio sources conflict: LG TV uses the external rendition for audio but the video renderer freezes while the muxed audio track confuses the TS demuxer. Symptom: audio plays, timeline advances, video image frozen.

**How to apply:** Only in the `isVariant = false` branch of `computeRelayM3u8` (CDN returns a multi-quality master rather than a single variant). The `isVariant = true` synthetic-master path is unaffected — it already routes video through `/as-va` (strips extra audio PIDs from TS) and audio through `/as-audio-pl` (properly demuxed per-language), so there is no muxed/external conflict there.

## noAudioProbe guard (companion fix)

Added `noAudioProbe=1` query param to every playlist URL generated by `computeRelayM3u8`'s `proxyUrl()`. The `/m3u8` handler skips its TS audio probe when this flag is set, preventing it from replacing a variant playlist with a synthetic master (which would be valid for `isVariant = false` case but adds latency and the outer master already defines the correct structure). Condition in `/m3u8` handler: `!doAudioFilter && !noAudioProbe`.

## CDN structure observed

- `isVariant = false` CDN master: 4 quality variants (240p/360p/480p/720p) + 5 language audio renditions (Hindi/Japanese/English/Malayalam/Tamil). Video TS has single muxed audio PID 256 (Hindi). Audio probe on 240p variant finds 0–1 tracks (inconsistently, network-dependent).
- `isVariant = true` CDN variant: single segment playlist, TS has multiple audio PIDs → probe finds all tracks → synthetic master with `/as-audio-pl` renditions + `/as-va`-routed video variant.
